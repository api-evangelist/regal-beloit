# Regal Beloit

Regal Beloit was a global manufacturer of electric motors, electrical motion controls, power generation, and power transmission products. In October 2021 Regal Beloit merged with Rexnord's Process & Motion Control segment to form Regal Rexnord Corporation. Regal Beloit's major brands — Marathon, Leeson, Genteq, and Browning — continue operating under Regal Rexnord.

**Website:** [https://www.regalrexnord.com](https://www.regalrexnord.com)
